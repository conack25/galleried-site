features

your gallery, your rules.

live rotation
set your interval from 15 minutes to once a day. galleried rotates quietly in the background, no interruptions.
your posts, cached locally
images live on your device. no uploads, no cloud, no surprises. completely private by design.
minimal, native ui
a compact 420px window that sits quietly at the edge of your workflow. no bloat, no clutter.
smart filters pro
filter by hashtag, minimum likes, or skip videos entirely. show only your best work.
multi-monitor pro
sync your gallery across every display. all monitors, one feed.
any account pro
follow any public instagram account as your wallpaper source. not just your own.
