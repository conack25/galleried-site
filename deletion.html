data deletion

last updated may 2026

galleried stores all data locally on your device. no data is stored on galleried servers. you can delete your data at any time using either of the methods below.

option 1 — delete from within the app

  1. open galleried
  2. go to settings
  3. scroll to the account section
  4. tap disconnect instagram

this immediately removes all cached images and stored credentials from your device.

option 2 — uninstall the app

uninstalling galleried from your device removes all locally stored data, including cached images and credentials.

further requests

if you have questions about your data or need further assistance, contact us at hello@galleried.app and we will respond within 5 business days.
